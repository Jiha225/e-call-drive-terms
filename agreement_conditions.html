E-Call Drive Terms and Conditions

1. Vehicle and Personal Conduct:

  • Maintain your vehicle in good operating condition and comply with all safety and regulatory standards. Only use the vehicle registered with E-Call Drive for bookings.
  • Follow hygiene protocols and ensure your vehicle is clean and smoke-free. Regular sanitization and maintaining a clean environment are mandatory.
  • Respect the privacy of passengers. Personal data (e.g., name, mobile number, address) must be kept confidential and not shared or disclosed without consent.

2. Prohibited Actions:

  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs is strictly forbidden. Possession of illegal substances, open containers of alcohol, or weapons in the vehicle is not allowed.
  • Discrimination against passengers based on race, religion, nationality, disability, sexual orientation, gender, or age is prohibited. Canceling or refusing rides based on these characteristics is unacceptable.

3. Service Quality:

  • Honor bookings that you accept. Unreasonable cancellations or transferring jobs to someone else without permission are not allowed.
  • Be polite and well-mannered in your interactions with passengers. Appropriate attire and a respectful attitude are required at all times.
  • Ensure a seamless experience for passengers, including timely pick-ups and drop-offs at the correct locations, and adherence to the agreed route unless changes are discussed with the passenger.

4. Legal and Safety Requirements:

  • Adhere to all traffic regulations, including speed limits and seatbelt or helmet use. Driving without a valid license or insurance is not permitted.
  • Notify E-Call Drive and authorities if you suspect the contents of a parcel for delivery might be illegal or dangerous.

These guidelines are designed to ensure safety, reliability, and high-quality service for all E-Call Drive users and partners.

By continuing with your application, you agree to abide by these Terms and Conditions.
